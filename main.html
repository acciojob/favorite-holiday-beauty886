Manali


Manali is a beautiful hill station nestled in the Himalayas, known for its scenic beauty, adventure sports, and peaceful environment.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Hadimba Temple – A unique wooden temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  2. Solang Valley – A popular spot for skiing and paragliding.
  3. Rohtang Pass – Offers stunning views and snow adventures.
  4. Old Manali – A peaceful village known for cafes and vibrant culture.
  5. Manu Temple – A spiritually significant site near the river.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A steamed bread with spicy filling.
  • Trout Fish – A local delicacy cooked in spices.
  • Babru – A Himachali version of stuffed puri.
  • Madra – A traditional dish made with chickpeas.
  • Mittha – A sweet dish made with dry fruits.
