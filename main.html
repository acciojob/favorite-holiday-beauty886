Manali


Manali is a picturesque hill station located in the Indian state of Himachal Pradesh. Known for its snow-capped mountains, lush green valleys, and adventure sports, it's a perfect getaway for nature lovers and thrill-seekers.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Solang Valley – Famous for paragliding and snow activities.
  2. Rohtang Pass – A scenic mountain pass with breathtaking views.
  3. Hadimba Temple – A unique temple surrounded by cedar forests.
  4. Old Manali – Known for its hip cafes and chilled vibes.
  5. Manu Temple – A spiritually significant and peaceful spot.

Must-Try Foods

  • Siddu – A steamed bread with spicy filling.
  • Trout Fish – A local delicacy cooked fresh from the rivers.
  • Babru – A Himachali version of stuffed puri.
  • Madra – A traditional dish made with chickpeas and yogurt.
  • Mittha – A sweet dish made with dry fruits and rice.
