Paris


Paris, the capital of France, is renowned for its art, architecture, and café culture. Often called the City of Light, it blends history with modern charm.

Top 5 Attractions

  1. Eiffel Tower — the most iconic landmark of Paris.
  2. Louvre Museum — home to the famous Mona Lisa.
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral — a Gothic masterpiece on the Île de la Cité.
  4. Champs-Élysées — one of the most famous avenues in the world.
  5. Seine River Cruise — a romantic way to see illuminated landmarks.

Must-Try Foods

  • Croissant — buttery and flaky breakfast classic.
  • Baguette — crisp crust with a soft interior.
  • Macarons — colorful almond meringue cookies.
  • Crêpes — thin pancakes with sweet or savory fillings.
  • French Onion Soup — rich broth topped with melted cheese.
